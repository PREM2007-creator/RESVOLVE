Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1 Information Collected

The Portal may collect:

  • Personal Data: Names, emails, phone numbers, addresses (for volunteers/staff).
  • Operational Data: Volunteer hours, resource allocations, donation records.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, access logs (for security).

2 How Data is Used

  • To manage volunteer engagements and resource distribution.
  • To communicate important updates.
  • To improve Portal functionality.

3 Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Data is not sold to third parties.
  • Limited sharing may occur with trusted partners (e.g., cloud service providers) under confidentiality agreements.
  • Disclosure may be required by law (e.g., court orders).

4 Data Security

  • Encryption and access controls protect sensitive data.
  • Regular security audits are conducted.
  • Users must report suspected breaches immediately.

5 User Rights

  • Users may request access to their data.
  • Corrections can be requested if data is inaccurate.
  • Account deletion requests will be processed, subject to legal retention requirements.

6 Cookies & Tracking

  • The Portal may use cookies for session management.
  • Users can disable cookies but may lose some functionality.

7 Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised. Users will be notified of significant changes.
